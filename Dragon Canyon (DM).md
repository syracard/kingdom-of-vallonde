# Politics
**Leadership:** The Council of Seven
	The Council of Seven is a group of representatives who are selected to run the affairs of Dragon Canyon. They hold elections every 7 years. 
	**Goals:** Improve conditions for all people.
	**Methods:** Supporting and financing local business and trade.
